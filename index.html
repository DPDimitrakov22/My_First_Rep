Caesar Salad

This classic salad is made with romaine lettuce, croutons, Parmesan cheese, and a creamy dressing made with anchovies, garlic, egg yolks, lemon juice, and olive oil.

Greek Salad

This Mediterranean salad is made with tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, olives, and a dressing made with olive oil, lemon juice, and herbs like oregano and thyme.

Cobb Salad

This American classic is made with lettuce, avocado, bacon, chicken, hard-boiled eggs, tomatoes, and blue cheese, and usually served with a vinaigrette dressing.
